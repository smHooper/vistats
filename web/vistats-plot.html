IRMA Stats

A wide array of visitor use metrics as reported to the national IRMA Stats repository. Queryies are limited to monthly or yearly intervals.

Backcountry Use

Number of users or usernights by backcountry unit, including backcountry permits issued by Talkeetna ranger station staff. Data are available back to 2001.

Denali and Foraker

Number of users or usernights on Denali and/or Foraker entered in the mountaineering permit database by Talkeetna staff. Data are available back to 2010.
